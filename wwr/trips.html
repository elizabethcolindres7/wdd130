Rogue River

Rogue River, Oregon, USA


We offer a thrilling rafting adventure amidst stunning landscapes and abundant wildlife. Rafters can embark on a multi-day journey through the rugged Rogue River Canyon, navigating class III and IV rapids while soaking in panoramic views of towering forests, cascading waterfalls, and ancient rock formations.


Futaleufú River

Futaleufú River, Patagonia, Chile


The Futaleufú River provides an adrenaline-fueled journey for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate challenge. Amidst the awe-inspiring beauty of Patagonia's untouched wilderness, adventurers can immerse themselves in a once-in-a-lifetime rafting experience that promises to leave them exhilarated.


Zambezi River

Zambezi River, Victoria Falls, Zambia


Rafters can embark on an adrenaline-charged expedition through the Batoka Gorge, tackling legendary class IV and V rapids amidst a backdrop of towering cliffs and lush riverine forests. As the river roars through narrow chasms and tumultuous rapids, adventurers are treated to unparalleled views of the spectacular Victoria Falls, with rainbows dancing in the mist. A rafting journey on the Zambezi River is not just an adventure—it's a thrilling encounter with the untamed forces of nature that promises to leave a lasting impression on all who dare to conquer waters.
